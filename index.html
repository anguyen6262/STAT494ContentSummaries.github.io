Genetics Through Statistical Analysis

Our Mission

L% seeks to explain the field of Genetic Statistics through exploring key research papers in the field. We will explain core concepts within the fields of Genetics and Statistics. This knowledge is crucial in in explaining the complex concepts in Statistic Genetics.

What is Genetic Statistics?

Genetic Statistics explores the relationship between ancestry and certain genetic traits.Statistic Genetics studies genetics with statistical methods and tools. Some software used are Rstudio and Python. They use libraries such as PLINK to generate amazing graphics. The most commone tool is Genome-wide association studies(GWAS) which is the study of genetic variants in individuals to see if that variant is associated with a trait.
