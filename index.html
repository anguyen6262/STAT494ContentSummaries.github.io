What is Genetic Statistics?

Genetic Statistics explores the relationship between ancestry and certain genetic traits using statistical methods and tools. Examples of these tools include software such as Rstudio and Python. With Rstudio, libraries such as PLINK can be used to generate PCA, QQPlots, and other graphics. Another important tool to mention is Genome-wide association studies(GWAS) which is the study of genetic variants in individuals to see if that variant is associated with a trait.
