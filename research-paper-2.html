Resetting the bar: Statistical significance in whole-genome sequencing-based association studies of global populations

Minor allele frequency: the amount of times a minor allele is found in a given population over time. Snps Array: A container of holds snp positions mixed with DNA to determine the alleles of the snps on that array. Genomic Region: A place where chromatin states can be detected. Principal component analysis: A collection of points that summarizes large data tables. Exome: sequence of exons in a sequence. Singular nucleotide variant (SNV): Haplotypes: group of alleles inherited from a single parent. recombination map: a linkage map based on frequencies
